About BHOLIF

Our Story

Buddu Hopeful Living Foundation Uganda Ltd (BHOLIF) is a nonprofit organization established on August 1st, 2023, and fully registered as a Company Limited by Guarantee on July 10th, 2025.

Founded by Rev. Father Ronald Mujabi of Masaka Diocese and Mr. Kirabira Tadeo (Proprietor of St. Francis Nursery and Junior Schools), BHOLIF represents the Luganda word "BUDDUKIRO" - meaning a place of hospitality, accommodation for the needy, and refuge.

We are dedicated to improving the welfare, social wellbeing, and healthy living of elderly and vulnerable people throughout Uganda, with a focus on economic empowerment, healthcare access, and community support.

Our Mission

To help the needy and elderly who are abandoned and are in vulnerable state as we give them the basic utilities and refreshing of their dignity.

Our Vision

To maintain hope and happiness in the hearts of the vulnerable people. "Okukuumira abayinike mu ssanyu ne mu ssuubi"

Our Objectives

Community Awareness

To enlighten society about elderly needs, regarding physical, psychological, social and economic needs and the secondary audiences.

Economic Empowerment

To ensure economic empowerment and sustainability of people in Buddu and other parts of the country.

Livelihood Improvement

To improve livelihood of elderly people in the community through comprehensive support programs.

Welfare Enhancement

To ensure improved welfare among vulnerable women and men through targeted interventions.

Sanitation & Water

To ensure proper sanitation through providing safe and clean water in communities with water problems.

Justice & Protection

To provide an avenue where elderly can report issues, bringing perpetrators to book by engaging the justice system.

Guidance & Counseling

To provide guidance and counseling to elderly and affected guardians going through mental and physical related issues.

Knowledge Sharing

To gather and disseminate knowledge about mental health and other rights for community development.

Partnership Development

To establish partnerships with Ugandan private sector organizations for educational planning and health services.

Our Services

Healthcare Access

Helping elderly and vulnerable individuals access essential healthcare services and medical support.

Welfare Improvement

Improving living conditions and welfare standards for vulnerable community members.

Companionship

Reducing loneliness through community programs and regular visits to isolated individuals.

Rights Protection

Fighting for the rights and protection of vulnerable people in our community.

Legal Support

Assisting elderly individuals in accessing legal and social services they need.

Community Care

Building a supportive community network for ongoing care and assistance.

Water & Sanitation

Providing safe and clean water access to communities facing water challenges.

Mental Health Support

Offering guidance and counseling for mental health and psychological wellbeing.

Economic Programs

Implementing donor-funded initiatives for sustainable economic empowerment.
